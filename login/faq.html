    keyboard_arrow_right SirHurt is crashing! It's not working!

    The most common cause is either because ROBLOX updated, or because you didn't install the C++ redistrubtals marked in the download section (or they were corrupted during install). If you have them installed, search for 'Control Panel', click 'Programs', and open up 'Programs and Features'. Find both 'Microsoft C++ Redistrubtals' in the list, select one, select 'Change' and then click Repair on the prompt. Do this for all installed redistrubtals. Then close SirHurt, and reopen the bootstrapper with administrator permissions and try using SirHurt again.
